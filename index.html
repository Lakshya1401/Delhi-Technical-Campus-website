Courses

These are the following courses that we offer

B.Tech

Our B.Tech programs are designed to provide comprehensive education in cutting-edge technologies. Students can choose from specializations such as Computer Science, Electrical Engineering etc.

BCA

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at Delhi Technical Campus is a dynamic and comprehensive undergraduate course designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer applications.

B.Arch

A dynamic 5-year program at Delhi Technical Campus, shaping creative minds into skilled architects. Blend design, technology, and sustainability for innovative spatial solutions. Explore your architectural passion with us.

BHMCT

A 4-year program at Delhi Technical Campus, crafting hospitality leaders. Blend theory and hands-on experience for thriving careers in hospitality.
