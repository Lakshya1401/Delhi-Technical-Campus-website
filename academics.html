BTech

Delhi Technical Campus offers a B.Tech course in various specializations such as Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, and Electronics & Communication Engineering.Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a comprehensive four-year degree program designed for individuals aspiring to build a rewarding career in the field of technology and engineering. In the current era of rapid technological advancements, acquiring a B.Tech degree is crucial for those who wish to thrive in the dynamic world of innovation and engineering. This professional course is tailored to impart a strong foundation in engineering principles, technological skills, and industry knowledge.Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) at Delhi Technical Campus is a four-year program designed for those aspiring to excel in technology and engineering. Our curriculum offers a solid foundation in engineering principles, programming languages, data structures, networking, and project management. Through practical applications and workshops led by industry experts, students stay abreast of the latest advancements. The program emphasizes emerging technologies and instills essential soft skills, ensuring graduates are well-equipped for the rapidly evolving tech industry. With a focus on teamwork and communication, our B.Tech graduates are prepared for careers in leading multinational corporations or advanced studies like M.Tech or MBA (Technology Management). The eight-semester course provides a transformative learning experience, setting the stage for success in the dynamic world of technology.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) is a three year degree course for the youth who are interested in making their career in the ﬁeld of Info-tech. It is a professional course to impart computer skills and knowledge. In this era of automation it becomes necessary to learn Information Technology with full enthusiasm. The BCA course under GGSIPU has been designed in such a manner that it provides a strong foundation in learning computer skills and students are exposed to various areas of computer applications including the latest developments in the industry. At DTC, we impart practical knowledge of the subjects to learn more about the real life situations from IT industries. We organize workshops and short term programmes on latest technologies by the eminent personalities from the academia and corporate world. The fast growing information technology and communication systems have become critical components of almost every company’s strategic plan. BCA is the best course to get an early in the ﬁeld of software development or web development. During the three years, we provide students the knowledge of database management system, web development tools, programming languages, networking, operating system, principle of management and principle of accounting etc. Our course prepares the students for advanced career in Computer Applications and grooms the students with intensive inputs from the rapidly changing IT industry & prepares to take up the challenges of the corporate world. The main emphasis is on building strong foundation needed for career options in Computers, focus on emerging Computer Applications. The IT industry has demand of skilled resources. We provide personality development classes to our students to strengthen their communication skills because to move ahead in this ﬁeld a strong analytical ability and excellent written and verbal communication skills are required. Excellent team working and peer to peer interaction are must to succeed in the IT industry. The successful completion of BCA opens up options in leading MNCs, IT Industries and Courses such as MCA, MBA (IT) etc. The programme is divided into six semesters.

BARCH

The Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) program at Delhi Technical Campus spans five enriching years, offering a comprehensive exploration of the art and science of architectural design. Tailored for those passionate about shaping the built environment, our curriculum blends architectural theory with hands-on practical experiences. Students delve into subjects like design principles, sustainable architecture, urban planning, and construction technology. Our program places a strong emphasis on fostering creativity, critical thinking, and technical proficiency.Through studio projects, workshops, and interactions with industry professionals, students gain a nuanced understanding of architectural trends and practices. The B.Arch program at Delhi Technical Campus encourages students to develop a unique design language while addressing contemporary challenges in architecture.Recognizing the significance of collaboration and effective communication in the architectural field, we integrate interpersonal and presentation skills into our coursework. Graduates emerge not only as skilled architects but also as adept communicators and problem solvers.Upon successful completion, B.Arch graduates can pursue diverse career paths, including roles in architectural firms, urban planning agencies, or further studies at the postgraduate level. The program spans ten semesters, providing a holistic education that prepares students for the complexities and opportunities within the dynamic realm of architecture."

BHMCT

The Bachelor of Hotel Management and Catering Technology (BHMCT) program at Delhi Technical Campus is a dynamic four-year course designed for individuals passionate about the hospitality and culinary industry. Our comprehensive curriculum covers a spectrum of subjects, including hotel operations, food and beverage management, hospitality law, and event management. Through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical training, students gain hands-on experience in hotel and kitchen operations. At Delhi Technical Campus, we prioritize industry relevance, integrating workshops and sessions led by experienced professionals. The program emphasizes the latest trends and technologies in hospitality, preparing students for the evolving landscape of the field. Soft skills such as communication, customer service, and teamwork are also integral components, ensuring graduates are well-rounded hospitality professionals. Upon completion, BHMCT graduates are equipped for diverse career paths in hotels, resorts, event management companies, or can pursue advanced studies in hospitality management. The program spans eight semesters, providing a comprehensive education that instills a deep understanding of the hospitality sector and the skills needed for success in this dynamic and customer-centric industry.
